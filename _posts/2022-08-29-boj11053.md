## 3.코드

```python
import sys
input = sys.stdin.readline

N=int(input())

p=list(map(int,input().split()))

dp=[0]*N
dp[0]=1

for i in range(1,N):
  for j in range(i):
    if(p[j]<p[i] and dp[j]>dp[i]):
      dp[i]=dp[j]
  dp[i]+=1

print(max(dp))
```
